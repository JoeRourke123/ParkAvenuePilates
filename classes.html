Information about

1:1 Sessions
We will work together to produce a bespoke program specific to your own circumstances. This program would be delivered one-to-one and provide home exercises which we will continue to develop to ensure you are progressing towards to your aims and goals. These sessions can take place in the comfort of your own home or in one of the below studios.
1:1 Sessions cost £50/hr.
Before you join
You must complete a mandatory pre-class assessment. This will provide and opportunity for you to discuss your aims and goals and complete a health questionnaire. I will introduce you to the key elements and fundamental movements of Pilates and identify any specific problems.
The assessment costs £35.
What to wear
Comfortable clothing that you can move easily in is advised. Shoes are not required, most clients like to work in socks or bare feet. All equipment is provided.
Cancellation Policy
All sessions must be paid in advance. If you cannot attend please advise more than 24 hours prior. Any cancellations less than 24 hours must be paid in full. If classes have been pre-booked they cannot be refunded but where possible an alternative class can be offered.

Prices

Sessions are pre-paid each half term based on the number of weeks, £66 for a six-week half term (£11 per session)

Flexible weekly payments are available at £13 per session but must be booked in advance and space cannot be guaranteed.
